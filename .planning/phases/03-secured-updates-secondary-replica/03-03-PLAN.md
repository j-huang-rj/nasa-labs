<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| primary authoritative service -> secondary slave zones | Replicated authoritative data crosses hosts and must remain read-only on the replica |
| external clients -> secondary replica | Queries must read replicated data only; writes and further transfers must stay blocked |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-03-05 | T | Secondary zone inventory and slave storage | mitigate | Mirror the live primary zone names/views exactly, but force every secondary entry to `type: slave` with `masters: [172.16.1.53]` and no update/transfer directives. |
| T-03-06 | R | Replication timing verification | mitigate | Use a signed update on the primary plus an `until`-based poll against `172.16.0.53` capped at 10 seconds so the plan detects slow or missing NOTIFY/transfer behavior instead of assuming eventual consistency. |
</threat_model>
